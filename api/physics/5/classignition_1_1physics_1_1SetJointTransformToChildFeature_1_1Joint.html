◆ SetTransformToChild()

void SetTransformToChild ( const Pose &  _pose)

Set the transformation matrix from the tip of the joint transform to the child link. The final result of Joint::GetTransform() will be.

GetTransformFromParent() * T(q) * GetTransformToChild()

where T(q) is the transform produced by the current generalized positions of the joint.

Parameters
[in]_poseThe desired transformation matrix
